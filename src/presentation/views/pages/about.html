Our Story

From Soil to Soul

We believe food is more than nourishment — it's memory, love, and connection to the land.

A kitchen rooted in tradition

Daily Taaza began in a small home kitchen in Hyderabad, where our founder watched her grandmother grind batter on stone every dawn — the rhythmic sound of tradition that shaped every meal.

Today, we carry that same devotion into every jar and packet we prepare. We partner with organic farms across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, stone-grind our batters the old way, and roast our spices in small batches over wood fire.

No factories. No shortcuts. Just real food made the way it always should be — with patience, care, and ingredients you can pronounce.
